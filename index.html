Lu Wang (王璐)

I am a professor in School of Software, Shandong University. I received my Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Shandong University of China in 2009. My research interests include photorealistic rendering, real-time rendering, material appearance modeling, and high-performance rendering.
